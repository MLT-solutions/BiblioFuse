### **Usage Guide**

1. **To Compress:**  
   * Navigate to the "Compress / Resize" tab.  
   * Click **Select File(s)**.  
   * Choose your image format (Keep JPEG for compatibility, WebP for max compression).  
   * Click **Start Processing**.
  
2. **To Merge (Pro Feature):**  
   * Navigate to the "Merge" tab.  
   * Add files and reorder them using the **Move Up/Down** buttons.  
   * Click **Merge Files**. The output will be saved in the source directory as \_merged.epub.
